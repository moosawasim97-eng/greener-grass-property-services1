Greener Grass Property Services is a locally owned and operated lawn care business serving Slidell, New Orleans and the surrounding areas. Reliable, professional, detail oriented — every visit, every time.

Our Story

Two Locals.
One Standard.

Greener Grass Property Services was founded by Ethan Barton and Zion Barrow, two property professionals who believe lawn care shouldn't be complicated, overpriced, or inconsistent. We started this business because we saw too many homeowners in Slidell and New Orleans dealing with unreliable crews, missed appointments, and sloppy work.

Our approach is simple: show up when we say we will, do the job properly every single time, and treat every property like it's our own. That commitment to consistency is what separates a genuinely good lawn care service from a forgettable one.

We are proudly local. We know the soil, the grass types, the seasonal patterns, and the climate challenges that come with maintaining a property in southeast Louisiana. That local knowledge means we treat your lawn the right way for this region, not a generic one-size-fits-all approach.
