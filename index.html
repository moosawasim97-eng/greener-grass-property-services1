What We Do

Our Services

From routine lawn care to full yard cleanups — Greener Grass Property Services keeps your property looking its absolute best, every single visit.

Lawn Mowing

Consistent, clean cuts on a schedule that fits your property. We treat every lawn like it's our own — sharp lines, even height, no shortcuts.

Edging

Crisp, defined edges along driveways, walkways, and flower beds that instantly elevate the look of your entire property.

Weed Eating

Trimming around fences, beds, posts, and tight corners that mowers can't reach — leaving nothing overlooked.

Hedge Trimming

Clean, precise shaping for hedges and shrubs that keeps your landscaping looking sharp and well maintained year round.

Yard Cleanups

Full property cleanups — leaves, debris, overgrowth, and seasonal mess cleared so your yard looks fresh and ready.

Ongoing Maintenance

Recurring service plans that keep your property consistently maintained without you having to think about it.
